Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

A Visionary Scientist and Statesman

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, born on October 15, 1931, and sadly departed on July 27, 2015, was a remarkable individual who left an indelible mark on India and the world.

He was not only a visionary scientist and a brilliant engineer but also a beloved statesman who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

Dr. Kalam's contributions to India's space and missile programs, including his leadership in the development of the Pokhran-II nuclear tests, demonstrated his unwavering dedication to the nation's scientific progress and security.

His simple living, high thinking, and commitment to education continue to inspire generations. His famous quote, "Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts, and thoughts result in action," resonates with people worldwide.

Dr. Abdul Kalam's legacy lives on, reminding us to dream big, work hard, and contribute to the betterment of society.
